What does this show?

For each kanji, you'll see interactive charts showing:

  • Solo: Used as a single-character name
  • Initial: Appears at the start of names
  • Middle: Appears in the middle (for names with 3+ characters)
  • End: Appears at the end of names

The charts show trends over time for both male and female names in the current data source.
